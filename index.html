Tag Patrol List

These are the lists of Genius tags that I often monitor. This site is created for easy navigation.

  1. Malaysia
    The Malaysia tag should be used in every song that is from Malaysia, or from an artist who is from that country.
  2. Terjemahan Bahasa Melayu
    Tags that should be used on a page where you translate foreign language songs to Malay.
  3. Bahasa Melayu
    The tag Bahasa Melayu should be included in the metadata of any song, guide, and page created in Malay or that include a significant amount of text in that language.
